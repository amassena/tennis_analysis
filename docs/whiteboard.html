Strengths

  • 240fps slo-mo capture (high fidelity)
  • 3D camera-invariant biomech grades
  • Claude Opus 4.7 coaching summaries
  • Web-shareable links (any device)
  • 96.5% F1 shot detection
  • Self-healing iCloud auth
  • Pro comparison filmstrips
  • Cheap hosting (R2, no egress)

Gaps

  • Coaching takes 5–15 min after recording
  • No instant feedback during practice
  • Single-user (no friend feed)
  • No history / progression view
  • No remote coach workflow
  • Cloud-dependent (no offline mode)
  • Sharing limited to "send link"
  • No social features (likes, comments)

Local for SPEED

  • Live AR coaching during play
  • Instant per-shot grade in 2s
  • Quick-fix tip between points
  • Works offline at any court
  • Zero per-shot API cost
  • Voice synth feedback ("Bend knees more")

Cloud for VALUE

  • Deep biomech analysis (Opus quality)
  • Pro comparison library
  • Permanent shareable URLs
  • Cross-device history sync
  • Friend feed + reactions
  • Coach-student feedback loop
  • Long-term progression graphs
  • Backups + DR

Future User Journey

  1. Practice: Live AR coach on phone, A–F grade per shot in 2 seconds
  2. End of session: Auto-uploads to cloud while you walk to your car
  3. In car: Phone shows summary "32 BH, avg knee 118° (PR!)"
  4. 10 min later: Deep cloud coaching ready, share to friends
  5. Next day: Coach replied with voice memo on shot #14
  6. End of week: Progression graph shows knee bend +8° over 2 weeks
